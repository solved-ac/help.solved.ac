---
title: Future Plans Following the Shutdown of BOJ
date: 2026-04-15
---

Hello! This is **solved.ac**, the community powered guide for programming challenges.

Today, it was unfortunately announced that [BOJ will be shutting down](https://www.acmicpc.net/board/view/165799).

- The integration between BOJ and solved.ac will end on April 28 of this year.
- Difficulty opinions and other data on programming problems remaining on solved.ac will continue to be provided in some form after April 28.
- Effective immediately, Stardusts earned from solving problems and contributing on solved.ac are increased by 10x.

We will share specific details about upcoming changes in follow-up announcements.
